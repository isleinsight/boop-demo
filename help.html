Need Help?

How do I send or request money?

Use the "Send / Request" page to look up a user, enter the amount, and choose whether to send or request. You'll receive confirmation once it's complete.

Where do I see my transactions?

All transactions are listed in the "Activity" section. You can filter by date or search for specific names or amounts.

Is BOOP secure?

Yes! BOOP uses end-to-end encryption, two-factor authentication, and secure Firebase authentication for all users.

How do I transfer to my bank?

On the Wallet page, tap "Transfer" and enter your amount. Bank transfer support is currently in beta and will be rolling out soon.

I can’t find someone in search

Make sure they have an account and are not currently on government assistance, as those accounts are restricted from transactions.
