Help & FAQs

Quick answers to common questions — and how to reach us.

Getting Started

A few basics to help you move money with confidence.
How do I send money?

Go to Send / Request, find the person, enter an amount, and tap Send. You’ll see a confirmation before it goes.

How do I request money?

On Send / Request, search for who you’re requesting from, enter the amount, and tap Request. They’ll get a notification in BOOP.

Where can I see my past activity?

Open Activity to review your recent transactions. Use the quick filters (7, 30, 90 days) or set a custom range. You can also export CSV.

Can I move money to my bank?

Not yet — bank transfers are coming soon.

I sent money to the wrong person. What should I do?

Contact Support immediately. If the funds haven’t been used, we may be able to help.

Still need help?

We’re here for you. Email support@boop.com or call (441) 555-1234.
