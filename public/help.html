Help & FAQs

How do I send money?
Go to the Send / Request page, search for another cardholder, enter the amount, and click Send.
How do I request money?
On the Send / Request page, search for a cardholder, enter the amount, and click Request.
Where can I see my past transactions?
Visit the Activity page to view your recent transactions and filter them by date range.
Can I transfer money to my bank?
Not at this time. Bank transfers will be available in a future update.
What if I sent money to the wrong person?
Please contact BOOP Support immediately. We may be able to help if the funds have not been used yet.
