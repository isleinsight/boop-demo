Help & Facts

Welcome to the BOOP Help page. Here you'll find quick answers to common questions and guidance on using your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add funds? You can add funds from your Wallet page by linking a payment method.
  • How do I send money? Go to "Send & Request" and search for the person you want to send funds to.
  • Can I request money from anyone? You can request money from any eligible cardholder.
  • Is BOOP secure? Yes, all transactions use bank-grade encryption and security measures.
