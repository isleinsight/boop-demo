Why a bracelet?

Phones lock. Cards get buried in wallets. A durable bracelet is visible, water‑resistant, and easy for responders to scan or read in the field.

Designed for Bermuda responders

Who can read it?

Emergency Medical Technicians and hospital staff with approved credentials. No one else can access your information.

What’s stored?

Only what you choose: allergies, medications, conditions, and an emergency contact. You can update this any time after activation.

What if I lose it?

We can disable the bracelet ID remotely and issue a replacement. Your data stays safe.
