Private by design

Your emergency profile is not public. Only authorised responders in Bermuda (ambulance and hospital staff) can unlock your information using their BOOP responder app. Your regular BOOP account cannot view or edit your medical notes without your permission.

Every access attempt is logged. You can opt out at any time.

How it works

1) Register interest

Fill out the form below. We’ll contact you to confirm eligibility and arrange your bracelet style/size.

2) Activate privately

We set up your secure profile with allergies, medications, conditions, and an emergency contact.

3) For emergencies only

EMTs/hospitals can scan the bracelet in an emergency to see your info — no one else can read it.

Pricing

HIP members: no monthly subscription — you only pay for the bracelet.

Everyone else: small monthly subscription plus bracelet cost. We’ll confirm prices when we contact you.
