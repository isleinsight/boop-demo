About BOOP

A smarter way to use assistance benefits—and a simpler way to accept them.

BOOP is an NFC payment card and platform for Bermuda. Cardholders spend benefits at participating vendors. Vendors get paid instantly with a flat 1% fee. Simple, transparent, and built for everyday life.

For cardholders

Use your BOOP Card to buy groceries and essentials at participating vendors. No hidden fees for you.

  • Tap just like any other card.
  • Only approved categories are allowed, so benefits go where they should.
  • 24/7 online balance and history.

For vendors

Flat 1% per transaction

BOOP is not Visa or Mastercard. We keep it simple—no 2.5–3.5% interchange surprises, no tiered pricing. Just a transparent 1% fee and next-day deposits*.

  • Fast onboarding and simple web tools.
  • Chargebacks are rare—transactions follow program rules.
  • Free listing in our vendor directory (opt-in).

How it works

  • Funds arrive on your BOOP Card via your program.
  • Shop at vendors that accept BOOP (look for the BOOP mark).
  • Compliance is built-in—restricted categories simply won’t go through.
  • Vendors get paid with a flat 1% fee and clear settlement.

Optional: Health & ICE swipe

You can choose to store a limited “In Case of Emergency” profile on your BOOP Card—like allergies, medications, and an emergency contact. With your consent, a clinic or ambulance can read this data by swiping your card to help in a crisis.

  • Opt-in only—you control what’s shared.
  • Read-only access for emergency providers.
  • Encrypted at rest and in transit.

Security & privacy

  • Modern encryption; credentials are hashed and salted.
  • Granular permissions for admins and auditors.
  • We never sell personal data. Period.

What BOOP is (and isn’t)

BOOP is a closed-loop payment network built for public programs. We’re not a bank, Visa, or Mastercard.

Eligibility and funding are provided by participating programs and agencies.

Frequently asked

Where can I use the BOOP Card?

At participating vendors that accept BOOP. We’re adding locations regularly—check the vendor list in your account (pilot access).

What does it cost?

Cardholders don’t pay fees. Vendors pay a flat 1% per transaction.

What happens if I lose my card?

Freeze it in your account or contact support. We’ll help replace it and protect your funds.
